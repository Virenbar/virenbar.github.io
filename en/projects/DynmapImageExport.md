Tool to download and merge Dynmap tiles into one image

![terminal](/assets/images/dynmap-image-export/terminal.gif){: .img-fluid}

#### Example

`.\DynmapImageExport m https://map.minecrafting.ru/ world flat [0,100,0] [5,6,5,5] 2`
